About
What is Paradox?
Paradox is a Google Chrome Extension aimed at improving privacy-based decision making. It displays tracking information, and compares this to the privacy policy of the websites you visit.
How can it help me?
Paradox puts you in control of your privacy by providing you with transparency of usage for your personal data. By using Paradox, you're in a better position to make privacy choices, to determine if your personal data is secure, and detect a range of privacy violations which might be affecting you.
Why was it made?
Paradox was created as part of a research project into analysing website trust with regards to user expectations.
What information does Paradox collect?
Absolutely none. Paradox is all about putting you in control of your own privacy, check out our Privacy Policy.
